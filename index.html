As Spring Festival is coming, I selected a poem that describes the phenomenon that happens during the spring festival for my first website blog.

元日
New Year's Day

[宋] 王安石
[Song Dynasty]Anshi,Wang
      爆竹声中一岁除，
      Another year is gone with the sound of the firecrackers.

      春风送暖入屠苏。
      Spring is coming, we can feel the warm wind. It is the time to drink tu su wine.

      千门万户曈曈日，
      On this bright new year’s day, thousands of families are busy.

      总把新桃换旧符。
      Every family are busy with changing the old scrolls and put up the new one.
